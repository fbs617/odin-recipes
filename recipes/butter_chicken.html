Butter Chicken Recipe

Description

Butter chicken, or murgh makhani, is a rich and creamy Indian dish featuring tender chicken simmered in a velvety tomato-based sauce. The chicken is typically marinated in yogurt and spices, then grilled or pan-seared before being added to a luscious sauce made with tomatoes, butter, cream, and aromatic spices like garam masala, cumin, and fenugreek. The result is a perfectly balanced dish that is mildly spiced, slightly tangy, and deeply flavorful.

Served with fluffy basmati rice or warm naan, butter chicken is a comforting and indulgent meal. Its creamy, mildly sweet taste appeals to a wide range of palates, making it a go-to for anyone exploring Indian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • Chicken (boneless, skinless)
  • Yogurt
  • Garlic and ginger
  • Garam masala
  • Cumin
  • Chili powder
  • Butter
  • Tomatoes (pureed)
  • Heavy cream
  • Fenugreek (kasuri methi)
  • Salt and sugar

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken in yogurt, garlic, ginger, and spices for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Cook the chicken in a pan until lightly charred, then set aside.
  3. In the same pan, melt butter and cook pureed tomatoes with spices until thickened.
  4. Stir in cream and fenugreek, then add the cooked chicken back to the sauce.
  5. Simmer for a few minutes until the chicken is tender and coated in the sauce.
  6. Serve hot with rice or naan.
